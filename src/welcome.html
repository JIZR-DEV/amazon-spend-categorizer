Thanks for installing!

Categorize your Amazon purchases locally using keyword/ASIN rules and mark each item as deductible and business/personal, without sending anything to the cloud.

How to start

  1. Go to your Amazon order history and click the extension icon to read orders from the page.
  2. Or export your order history CSV from Amazon and import it with the "Import CSV" button.

Your purchase data never leaves your device. Categorization rules can optionally sync across your devices.
